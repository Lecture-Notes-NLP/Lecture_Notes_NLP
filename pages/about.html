Our Mission

Our mission is to revolutionize the way students engage with lecture content by leveraging Natural Language Processing (NLP) and deep learning. We aim to develop an intelligent system that transcribes real-time speech, enhances readability with accurate punctuation, and identifies key phrases to streamline the note-taking process. Our Vision

We envision a future where technology seamlessly augments the educational experience, transforming the traditional approach to lecture documentation. Our goal is to create an adaptive and personalized learning environment where students can effortlessly access high-quality, structured, and interactive study materials. By advancing real-time transcription accuracy, optimizing keyphrase extraction, and ensuring seamless integration with online resources, we aspire to empower learners with an intuitive, AI-driven study companion that enhances comprehension and retention
